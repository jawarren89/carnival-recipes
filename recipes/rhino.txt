Alexandra's Sunday Pasta Sauce
------------------------------

Servings: 10

Directions:

Time: 4 hours 50 minutes

2 pounds pork spareribs
6 cloves garlic
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
1 pinch salt
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese
1 (1 inch thick) slice beef shank
2 bone-in chicken thighs
2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
1 onion, diced
3 (28 ounce) cans crushed Italian (plum) tomatoes (such as San Marzano)
1/4 cup tomato paste


1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
3. Place beef, pork, and chicken in pan and turn to coat with olive oil.
4. Roast in the preheated oven until meat is well browned, 20 to 30 minutes.
5. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
6. Cook and stir onion with a pinch of salt in hot oil until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
8. Pour crushed tomatoes, 1 1/2 cups water, and tomato paste into onion mixture.
9. Add roasted beef, pork, and chicken to tomato sauce mixture.
13. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, and simmer gently until sauce reduces and meat is tender, about 4 hours.
